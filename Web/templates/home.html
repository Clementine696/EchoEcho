ABOUT ECHOECHO

It's an app about noise cancellation.

MICROPHONE PAGE

There is a button for noise canceling and a section of the graph showing real-time values ​​when the noise canceling button is pressed. The graph will change from being a histogram of itself to a histogram when noise is eliminated. You can see the change from the graph and you can also listen to your own voice whether it's really better when the noise is cancelled.

SOUNDPAD PAGE

Initially, there will be default sounds within the app, but users can add more sounds as they want and can set hotkeys. that this sound will work when pressing any button Initially, you can choose whether to play the sound for yourself or play the sound through the microphone for others to hear.

VOICECHANGER PAGE

can be pressed to change the sound as specified and adjust various values ​​which can be exported and imported via the website.
