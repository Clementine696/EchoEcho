ABOUT ECHOECHO

It's an app about noise cancellation.

MICROPHONE PAGE

There is a button for noise suppression and a section of the graph that shows real-time speech when the noise suppression button is pressed. The graph shows a histogram of normal voice and suppressed voice. You can see the changes in the graph and you can also listen to your own voice to see if it really improved when the noise was reduced.

SOUNDPAD PAGE

There will initially be a default soundpad sound within the app. Users can add audio files as they wish. You can choose to listen to the audio or play it through the microphone for others to hear.

VOICECHANGER PAGE

Users can change the speaking effect according to the sound effects provided by the app.
